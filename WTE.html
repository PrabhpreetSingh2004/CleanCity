What is Waste to Energy?

Waste to Energy (WTE) is a cutting-edge process that converts everyday garbage into usable energy — electricity, heat, or fuel. It’s the perfect solution to tackle urban waste and energy demand at the same time.

The Problem & The Solution

The Problem

  • Overflowing landfills
  • Unsegregated waste
  • Methane & toxic emissions

The Solution

  • Burning waste in modern plants
  • Biogas from food/agri waste
  • Eco-friendly fuel from plastics

Types of Waste to Energy Technologies

🔥 Incineration

High-temp combustion reduces waste and generates steam to produce energy.

💩 Biogas

Organic waste digested by microbes to produce clean methane gas.

🧪 Pyrolysis

Heating waste without oxygen to produce fuel oil and gas.

🌫️ Gasification

Turning carbon-based waste into syngas, a usable fuel.
