Why Afforestation Matters

Afforestation is not just planting trees — it's a climate action, a biodiversity strategy, and a way to make our cities beautiful, clean, and resilient to change.

🌿 Clean Air

Trees reduce air pollution and bring fresh oxygen to urban neighborhoods.

💧 Water Retention

Forests help recharge groundwater and reduce the risk of floods.

🦜 Wildlife Shelter

Trees provide safe habitats for birds, bees, and small creatures.

☀️ Urban Cooling

Green spaces cool down heat islands, making cities more livable.
